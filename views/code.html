Start helping solve big problems

MapRejuice is only as good as the publishers and developers that opt-in to help out. If you're willing (and are able) to place our code on your page or project, you'll be helping to increase the overall computing power we have.

One should consider the ethical implications of putting this on a page - we purposefully keep data transfers small, to prevent eating up data on metered connections and the like. We also currently don't run on mobile unless you opt in, to avoid issues with eating up battery life in the background. We like to play by the "Golden Rule".

Will this impact performance of my site?

We've gone to great lengths to make sure that our computations don't mess with the user experience that you've created for your visitors or customers. Our method relies on using a new technology called Web Workers that separate our operations from your own.

If something seems unsafe, we hold off doing anything completely - your user experience still comes comes first, as there's more than enough machines at our disposal.
